MISSION DESCRIPTION:
Captain Sisko! A swarm of meteorites are coming towards the WORMHOLE! Starfleet has asked that you get into the DEFIANT and stop them!

CONTROLS:
Arrow Keys, or WASD to move.
Mouse 1, or Spacebar to fire.
